Project Overview

Introduction

Redsun2020: REVISE is an ambitious reimagining of the classic Redsun2020 mod for Deus Ex. Set in a near-future Tokyo, this storyline plunges players into a dark and gripping narrative of corporate power, underground resistance, and the struggle for survival. As J.C. Denton, you navigate through labyrinthine plots involving shadowy factions, powerful organizations, and a city on the brink of chaos. The adventure spans diverse locations, from the neon-lit streets of Shinjuku to the hidden depths of the Kurokuma hideouts, each with its own unique challenges and secrets.
